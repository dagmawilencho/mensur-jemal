About Me

Who is
Mensur Jemal

Mensur Jemal was born in Becho Kebele, Ilibabur, Ethiopia. He went to elementary school there. Following this, he attended his secondary school at Metu High School. After completing high school, he started his own business, which helped him become one of the leading entrepreneurs in Ethiopia. He was just eighteen years old when he began his own business. Mensur had worked as an Isuzu truck driver when he was younger. Mensur later traveled to Addis Ababa in search of new opportunities and perspectives after realizing the limitations of his rural upbringing.Mensur Jemal is a successful entrepreneur who, from humble beginnings, rose to become one of the most successful businesspeople in Ethiopia. He is a shining example of what can be achieved with hard work, perseverance, and a desire to succeed. Mensur is engaged in numerous business ventures, including bitcoin, consulting, delivery services, and others. Mensur is currently investing in a wide range of startups in Ethiopia.Mensur’s Jemal net worth is considered to be 112 million ETB (according to www.topethiopia.com). However, that number is likely to increase with the success of his ventures.
